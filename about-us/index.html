    About Us

    ABOUT STHIRA CONSTRUCTIONS

    Sthira Constructions believes in building outstanding projects at a competitive price and a commitment to be eco-friendly with minimum digression to the environment. A staunch believer of green construction, PDP always strives for customer satisfaction. Quality in every aspect of our business is the byword. We march into the future, with the confidence that we have gained and the experience we have gathered with every completed project. We are firmly committed to creating vibrant communities, where the residents can live, work and play in natural settings. Customer delight is the reward that we seek at all times. We ensure transparency and strive to meet project deadlines at all times. Our sole objective is to create landmarks and raise the bar of construction with every project and thereby provide customer delight.

    OUR VISION

    To be a pioneer in the field of construction; making dwelling a lifestyle statement, using the latest technological advancements for the benefit of the stakeholders.

    OUR MISSION

    To earnestly endeavor to utilise all the resources towards attaining the vision positively, progressively and in a sustained manner.
