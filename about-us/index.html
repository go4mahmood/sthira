    Company Information

    ABOUT STHIRA CONSTRUCTIONS

    Sthira is a leading provider of essential infrastructure services to governmental and commercial sector in Southern India.

    OUR STRATEGY

    Sthira's mission is to be recognized as the leading provider of essential infrastructure services in southern India. The company strategy is to deliver outstanding performance through a focus on innovation and customer service. The directors believe that this will lead to growth, building on existing market positions, by developing new markets and services and extending geography, both organically and through new project areas. We have in place an experienced and entrepreneurial management team, who have been in the business for over 10 years with senior consultations to support growth.

    QUALITY POLICY

    Our quality assurance and quality control cover all trading and contracting activities from conception, development, construction, production, installation,serving and documentation. it includes the regularization of raw materials, assemblies, products and components, service related construction management and inspection process.

    RISK MANAGEMENT

    Our company believes in operational control and risk management to be the key in continued growth and profitability of any enterprise. To that end, we manage our ticket size to be proportional to our company's net worth and operational capabilities. We believe in scaling our operations in a measured manner, without over-extending management or our balance sheet. Growth but not at the cost of stability.
